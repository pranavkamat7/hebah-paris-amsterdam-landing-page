The itinerary

9 days, city by city.

Every day is planned so you get the real version of each city and not the rushed, tick-the-box version. This is Europe at the right pace.

Day 01
Arrive in Amsterdam

Land at Schiphol, check in, and meet the group over dinner. Amsterdam at dusk hits differently, the canals glowing, everyone on bicycles like it's perfectly normal. It is. And it's perfect.

Arrival Hotel check in Group dinner
Day 02
Amsterdam city tour and Zaanse Schans

Dam Square, the Royal Palace, the Anne Frank House exterior. Then Zaanse Schans, actual windmills, actual cheese farm, all very real and very Dutch. End with a canal cruise that makes you feel like you're inside a postcard. Worth it every time.

Zaanse Schans Canal cruise Dutch culture
Day 03
Brussels stop and arrive in Paris

On to Paris via Brussels. Grand Place is genuinely one of the most beautiful squares you'll ever stand in and Manneken Pis is exactly as small as everyone warns you. Then Paris. You arrive, step outside the hotel, and immediately understand what all the fuss is about.

Brussels Grand Place Paris arrival
Day 04
Paris highlights

Place Vendôme, Opéra Garnier, Champs Élysées, Arc de Triomphe, a full guided day through the city that invented looking good. Then up to the second floor of the Eiffel Tower. Then the Seine cruise. You'll have opinions about Paris by the end of this day. Good ones.

Eiffel Tower Seine cruise City tour
Day 05
Disneyland Paris

Full day at Disneyland Paris. Yes, we're doing it. No, we're not apologising. Whether you're nine or twenty-nine on the inside, this is a day where nobody is too cool for anything. The best kind of day.

Disneyland Full day Group experience
Day 06
Paris to Frankfurt

Travel from Paris to Frankfurt. Check in and spend the evening in the Römerberg old town,a half-timbered buildings, cobblestone, the Main riverfront at golden hour. Frankfurt is the city people overlook and then can't stop talking about.

Frankfurt Römerberg Hotel check in
Day 07
Frankfurt to Zurich and Mt. Titlis

Travel to Zurich, then up to Mt. Titlis for snow you did not plan for emotionally. The revolving cable car, the glacier cave, the views that make your phone storage cry and this is what you will talk about and tell everyone.

Mt. Titlis Snow experience Cable car
Day 08
Zurich city tour

Zurich old town, Lake Zurich, Bahnhofstrasse, and the Rhine Falls, which are loud and spectacular and worth every second. Last evening together as a group. Good food, easy conversation, the specific kind of happy that comes at the end of a good trip.

Zurich Rhine Falls Lake Zurich
Day 09
Fly home from Zurich

Last breakfast in Zurich, check out, airport. You board the flight home well rested, well fed, and with enough photos to keep your camera roll busy for months. The deadpan face is optional.

Departure Last breakfast
